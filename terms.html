Purpose

The App is a personal automation tool that assembles and uploads the operator's own original short videos to the operator's own TikTok account through TikTok's official Content Posting API.

Acceptable use

The operator uses the App only to post their own content to their own account, in compliance with TikTok's Terms of Service, Community Guidelines, and Developer Terms. The App is not used to post on behalf of others, to spam, or to perform unauthorized automated actions.

Publishing control

By default the App uploads videos as drafts to the operator's TikTok inbox; the operator reviews and publishes them manually. No content is published without the operator's involvement.

AI-generated content

Videos that contain AI-generated media are labeled using TikTok's AI-content disclosure at the time of posting.

The App is provided "as is" for personal use, without warranty of any kind.
