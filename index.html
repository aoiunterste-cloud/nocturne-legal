This page covers the Privacy Policy and Terms of Service for the personal software tool ("the App") used by its individual operator to create and upload short motivational videos to the operator's own TikTok account through TikTok's official Content Posting API.

Privacy Policy

Who we are. The App is a personal, single-operator tool. It is not a multi-user service and does not offer accounts to third parties.

Data we access. With the operator's explicit OAuth authorization, the App uses the following TikTok scopes solely for the operator's own account:

  • video.upload — to send the operator's own videos to the operator's TikTok inbox/drafts for the operator to review and publish.
  • user.info.profile, user.info.stats, video.list — to read the operator's own profile and public video performance metrics (views, likes, follower count, etc.) for the operator's own analytics.

How we use it. Access tokens are used only to upload the operator's own content and to read the operator's own statistics. The App does not access, collect, sell, or share data about any other TikTok users.

Storage. OAuth access/refresh tokens are stored locally on the operator's own machine (in a permission-restricted file) and are never transmitted to any third party. No personal data is stored on external servers operated by the App.

Third parties. The App does not share data with advertisers or analytics providers. It communicates only with TikTok's official API endpoints.

Retention & deletion. Tokens persist locally until the operator deletes them or revokes access in their TikTok settings. Revoking the App in TikTok account settings immediately ends all access.

AI-generated content. Videos produced with the App that contain AI-generated media are labeled using TikTok's AI-content disclosure at the time of posting.

Children. The App is operated by an adult and is not directed to children.

Terms of Service

Purpose. The App is a personal automation tool that assembles and uploads the operator's own original short videos to the operator's own TikTok account.

Acceptable use. The operator uses the App only to post their own content to their own account, in compliance with TikTok's Terms of Service, Community Guidelines, and Developer Terms. The App is not used to post on behalf of others, to spam, or to perform unauthorized automated actions.

Publishing control. By default the App uploads videos as drafts to the operator's TikTok inbox; the operator reviews and publishes them manually. No content is published without the operator's involvement.

No warranty. The App is provided "as is" for personal use, without warranty of any kind.

Changes. These terms may be updated; the latest version is posted on this page.
