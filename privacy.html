Nocturne Motivation — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-27 · Contact: see the operator's TikTok profile. · Terms of Service

This Privacy Policy applies to the personal software tool ("the App") used by its individual operator to create and upload short motivational videos to the operator's own TikTok account through TikTok's official Content Posting API.

Who we are

The App is a personal, single-operator tool. It is not a multi-user service and does not offer accounts to third parties.

Data we access

With the operator's explicit OAuth authorization, the App uses the following TikTok scopes solely for the operator's own account:

  • video.upload — to send the operator's own videos to the operator's TikTok inbox/drafts for the operator to review and publish.

How we use it

Access tokens are used only to upload the operator's own content. The App does not access, collect, sell, or share data about any other TikTok users.

Storage

OAuth access/refresh tokens are stored locally on the operator's own machine (in a permission-restricted file) and are never transmitted to any third party. No personal data is stored on external servers operated by the App.

Third parties

The App does not share data with advertisers or analytics providers. It communicates only with TikTok's official API endpoints.

Retention & deletion

Tokens persist locally until the operator deletes them or revokes access in their TikTok settings. Revoking the App in TikTok account settings immediately ends all access.

AI-generated content

Videos produced with the App that contain AI-generated media are labeled using TikTok's AI-content disclosure at the time of posting.

Children

The App is operated by an adult and is not directed to children.
